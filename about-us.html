Our Company’s Story

YSK MAJU VENTURES SDN BHD or formerly known as YSK Maju BESTARI is a leading supplier of fresh produce serving restaurants and other foodservice operators in parts Kuala Lumpur and Selangor. Known as the “fresh food specialists”, YSK offers clients a comprehensive fresh produce solution to suit any business. With trained fresh food specialists hand selecting each item direct from the grower daily, customers can be guaranteed that only the best quality produce is sourced so that it arrives fresh to your door. With a wide selection of fresh fruit and vegetables, exotic tropical fruit and vegetables, Asian vegetables, processed fruit and vegetables, herbs, fresh salads and eggs, YSK is the answer to your fresh menu requirements.We do everyday delivery except on Sundays & National Public Holidays without imposing minimum order condition. In this way, we help our clients to save on storage and it will also eliminate wastages due to bulk storage. In events of ad hoc orders or emergencies, we will do our best to service our clients’ because we know the importance and urgency in this industry.
